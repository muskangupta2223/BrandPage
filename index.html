Our Eco Promise :

At Sofy, we're committed to providing period care that's gentle on your body and kind to the planet. That's why our Organic range features a 100% organic cotton top sheet certified by the Organic Content Standard (OCS), grown without harmful pesticides. Free from chlorine, synthetic dyes, fragrances, and super-absorbent polymers, our pads are dermatologically tested and certified “Excellent” by Dermatest, Germany—making them safe even for sensitive skin. With a breathable design for all-day comfort and a strong focus on sustainability, our Eco Promise ensures that every Sofy pad offers the protection you need while supporting a healthier, greener future.
